About Me

I am currently a student at UC Berkeley's Extension Full Stack Web Developer Certificate Program. I have BS in Environmental Science and Chemistry from The Evergreen State College that I received in 2011. I have worked in various positions in laboratories including as a laboratory superviser however I never felt like working in a lab suited me. I have always been fascinated with design and development and been looking for a way into a career that would allow me to explore my passions more thoroughly.

Currently, I live in the Los Angeles with my husband Mark and my dog Christmas-Present where we have lived for the past 5 years. I met Mark while living in Seattle about 6 years ago and pretty quickly found out that share just about every interest (with the exception of music taste and television shows). I met my dog 13 years ago, when I realized I cannot live without a pet (or I don't know how to) and that I cannot keep a large German Shepherd in apartments, so I got a yorkie instead. He appears, so far, to be immortal.
